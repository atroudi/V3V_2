Online Demo for 2D-3D Conversion

        This demo converts uploaded 2D sports videos to 3D videos. More info about this project can be found here

Steps to convert 2D videos:
1- Choose a file to convert (recommended duration less than 5 minutes and in HD quality)
2- After choosing the video, enter your email address, then click the "Submit" button
3- A downloadable link for the result will be sent to your email after conversion
