Online Demo for 2D-3D Conversion

This demo converts uploaded 2D sports videos to 3D videos. More info about this project can be found here

Steps to convert 2D videos:
1- Choose a file to convert (recommended duration less than 5 minutes)
2- After choosing the video, click the "Submit" button
3- A downloadable link for the result will be displayed on the same page and it can be optionally sent to your email
